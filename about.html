About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a sense of community and cultural pride by organizing the annual Siddha Durga Puja. We aim to create an inclusive environment where traditions are celebrated and passed down to future generations. We are committed to organizing cultural events, social gatherings, and charitable activities throughout the year.

Our History

2010 - The Beginning

A small group of enthusiasts came together to organize the first Siddha Durga Puja in a local community hall.

2015 - Growth and Expansion

The celebration grew, attracting more families and moving to a larger venue to accommodate the growing community.

2020 - A New Chapter

We officially registered as a non-profit organization, expanding our activities to include year-round cultural and social events.
