About Us

Our Journey

Our cultural events have been the key attraction of our puja. From traditional rituals to vibrant performances, we strive to create an immersive experience for all attendees.

Our History

2021 - Lakeville Residents Durga Puja

A group of enthusiasts came together to organize the first Lakeville Durga Puja in our complex.

2022 - Rise in Enthusiasm

The celebration grew, attracting more families and moving to a larger venue to accommodate the growing community.

2023 - New Additions

We added Astami bhog and Kali Puja & Diwali within the scope of our subscription

2024 - New Residents

Many new residents joined in this year's celebration, enriching our community and bringing fresh perspectives.
